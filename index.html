WHO WE ARE

We are interior designers in Lviv that offer a bespoke service to create beautiful, functional spaces for residential clients, commercial firms and the retail and hospitality sectors. Also we offer our handmade unique furniture and interior accessories.

All of our projects are directed by our deep understanding of client’s needs. Whether we’re renewing the interior decoration or undertaking a complex residential renovation, we work closely with the client to develop a solution that reflects their needs and personality. Our approach leads to interior spaces that enhance the existing architecture, look absolutely stunning and bring pleasure to all who live and work there.
